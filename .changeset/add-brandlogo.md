Add BrandLogo component — filled brand/merchant logos (Spotify, Netflix, Visa, PayPal, Apple Pay, Cash App, and 28 more). Renders in currentColor by default or the official brand color via the `brand` prop; sized via `size`. Names exported as `brandNames`, official hexes as `brandColors`. Paths from Simple Icons (CC0).
